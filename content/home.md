# Welcome to the Safe to Stretch Toolkit 🧘

When people learn teamwork, leadership, or feedback skills at work, they need to feel safe to speak up and make mistakes. If employees of different ranks train in the same room, junior staff often stay silent because they are worried about looking bad in front of managers.

This toolkit provides prompts and tools for facilitators and learners. It uses simple methods and design choices to balance power in the room. This makes it easier for everyone to participate comfortably before, during, and after a session.

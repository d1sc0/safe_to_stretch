# Welcome to the Safe to Stretch Toolkit

When people participate in workplace training, they need to feel safe to speak up, ask questions, and make mistakes. When employees of varying levels of seniority train together, hierarchies can constrain everyone: junior staff may stay silent to avoid professional risk, while senior leaders may hold back to maintain an expectation of absolute expertise.

This toolkit provides prompts and tools for facilitators and learning designers to navigate these dynamics and build psychological safety in any learning environment. It aims to provide simple prompts and tools to help balance power in the room, making it easier for everyone to participate comfortably.

This is an early version of both the site and content. Content has been seperated into pages that reflect the considerations you may want to take [before](#before), [during](#during) and [after](#after) training.
